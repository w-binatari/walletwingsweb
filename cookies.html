1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences and improve your experience.

We use essential cookies for authentication and session management. We also use analytical cookies to understand how explorers interact with our voyage engine.

4. Managing Cookies

You can adjust your cookie preferences through your browser settings. Note that disabling essential cookies may impact the application's functionality.
