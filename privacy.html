Last Updated: December 2025

1. Data Collection

We collect information you provide directly to us when you create an account, create trip goals, or communicate with us. This includes your name, email, and financial targets for travel.

2. Usage of Data

Your trip data is used to provide you with accurate destination recommendations. We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We use anonymized, aggregated data to improve our community insights feature.

3. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time via your account settings or by contacting our support base camp.
